2019-2020

Master thesis @ Volvo cars

I am currently working on my Master thesis at Volvo Cars within the wheel suspension E & SW team. My thesis work is divided into two parts. The first part of the work is to study the influence of signal delay, accuracy and synchronization on the performance of the suspension control system.
